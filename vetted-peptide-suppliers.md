## WHAT TO VERIFY BEFORE BUYING

### Must-Haves ✅
- [ ] COA (Certificate of Analysis) from independent lab
- [ ] Purity 98%+
- [ ] Batch-specific testing (not generic)
- [ ] USA manufactured
- [ ] Third-party lab (not self-tested)

### Red Flags 🚩
- [ ] No COA available = walk away
- [ ] Refuses to share testing = suspicious
- [ ] Prices too low = likely low purity
- [ ] Generic COAs reused = lazy or fake
- [ ] No response to questions = don't trust

### How to Verify CoA
1. Check batch number matches your order
2. Verify testing lab is independent (Freedom Diagnostics, SGS, etc.)
3. Compare HPLC purity % to claimed %
4. Check molecular weight matches expected
