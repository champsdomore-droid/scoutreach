# Peptide Company Business Plan
## Research-Grade B2C Platform

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**Company Focus:** Sell research-grade peptides labeled "for laboratory research purposes only - not for human consumption"

**Target Market:** Biohackers, athletes, health enthusiasts, longevity researchers

**Competitive Advantage:** Trust, quality, transparency, athlete/coach network

---

## 2. Business Model

### Revenue Streams
| Product | Margin | Repeat Customer |
|---------|--------|------------------|
| Reconstituted vials | 60-70% | Yes (monthly) |
| Powder (bulk) | 40-50% | Yes (quarterly) |
| Bundles/stacks | 70-80% | Yes |
| Subscription | 75-85% | Locked in |

### Pricing Strategy
- **Entry point:** $50-80/vial (Reta, CJC, etc.)
- **Mid-tier:** $100-150/vial (stack combinations)
- **Premium:** $200+ (custom formulations)

---

## 3. Compliance Strategy

### "Research Purposes Only" Framing
- All products labeled: "For laboratory research only. Not for human consumption."
- Terms of Service: Explicitly states research use only
- Website: Research laboratory products, not supplements
- Packaging: Lab-grade labeling

### Legal Safeguards
- LLC structure with liability protection
- Terms of Service with mandatory acceptance
- Age verification (21+)
- No medical claims
- Disclaimer on all products

### Payment Processor Solutions
| Option | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| Stripe (sometimes) | May reject initially |
| Square | More lenient on supplements |
| Dedicated merchant accounts | $299+ setup, higher fees |
| Crypto (USDT) | Avoids bank issues entirely |
| Wire transfers | For bulk orders |

**Recommendation:** Start with Stripe + crypto fallback. Many peptide companies use this combo.

## 6. Fulfillment

### Dropshipping vs. In-House
| Option | Cost | Control |
|--------|------|---------|
| Third-party lab | Lower | Less |
| In-house inventory | Higher | Full |

**Recommendation:** Start with third-party lab fulfillment. Scale to inventory when sales hit $10k/month.

### Storage
- Refrigeration required
- Special shipping (ice packs, insulated)
- US-based only (avoid customs issues)
