## Sample Lesson

### 1.2 — The Skills You Already Have That Pay

You know more than you think. Most people stuck in the 9-to-5 don't see their own expertise because it's "just what they do." Here's how to find it.

---

**What you do at work isn't special. It's also incredibly valuable.**

As a school counselor, you know how to handle crisis situations, navigate parent conversations, manage emotions under pressure, and solve problems on the fly.

As a soccer coach, you know how to motivate teenagers, break down complex techniques into teachable moments, and run a training session that actually works.

These aren't "just" counselor skills or "just" coaching skills. These are **transferable skills** that people pay for.

---

**Your transfer test:**

Answer these four questions:

1. What do people ask you for advice about?
2. What have you done for others that they couldn't do themselves?
3. What would someone need to learn to do what you do?
4. What would you teach if you had to teach someone in 30 days?

---

**The money test:**

For each skill you identify, ask: *Would someone pay to learn this or have someone do this for them?*

- If yes → that's your offer
- If no → cross it off, keep looking

Most people find 3-5 skills that pass the test. Pick one.

---

**Pick one skill.** Write it down. We'll build your offer around it in the next lesson.
